About Us
Imagine how enriching it would be to learn about the experiences and accomplishments of the many who have departed while visiting a cemetery. QR My Ancestors enable users with a free account to create unique profiles pages for deceased individuals they love to share their life story. Each profile page will have a unique QR code that can be placed at a gravesite for visitors to easily scan and learn more about the story behind the name etched into the headstone. 

Qr My Ancestors was founded in 2022 by Alex Wirick, Kizy Matzenbacher, Marcus LaBar, and Amy Zhang. The idea of a QR code on a gravestone isn't new; however, the founders saw the need to use this technology and expand the opportunity to all U.S. states.  
Our ancestors are an important part of our lives. They ultimately surround us through their gravesites. The ability to understand their trials, their opportunities and their journeys provide invaluable knowledge.
